BiopartsDB

BiopartsDB is the main product of my master's thesis (except for the thesis itself) at the University of Edinburgh. It's a curated repository of synthetic biology parts libraries, with a bunch of the ones that I considered well-characterized enough.

The premise here is that synthetic biology parts are subject to too many variables from experiment to experiment, so parts from different works cannot be compared. Therefore, only big parts libraries can give you reliable quantitative information when comparing two different parts.

Phyrexian Dictionary

Phyrexian is a constructed language (conlang) created by Wizards of the Coast for a fictional world as part of their stories in the Magic: the Gathering card game. They introduced teh language in altered verisons of some cards, but without any translation guide.

After a lot of effort by the MtG community in decyphering the language, I decided to make a more organized and accessible version of the knowledge on the language (following versions 1 and 2 by Fernando Franco Felix, at HighlyEntropicMind on Youtube).
